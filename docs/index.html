Breaking Barriers with Technology

CACTUS is a groundbreaking achievement in assistive technology, being the first of its kind at the University of Malta, a national milestone, and a significant step on the international stage.

Inclusive by Design

CACTUS is an assistive technology created to empower individuals with severe motor challenges. It is specifically designed for those with conditions like ALS, locked-in syndrome, and spinal injuries, as well as stroke survivors because we believe that physical constraints should never be a barrier to mental freedom or the ability to connect with the world.
