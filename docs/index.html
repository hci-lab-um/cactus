Reimagining web browsing for people with severe motor limitations. Using advanced eye-tracking and adaptive switches, it transforms complex websites into gaze-friendly interfaces, making communication, work, and entertainment accessible to everyone, regardless of physical ability.

Breaking Barriers with Technology

CACTUS represents a breakthrough in assistive technology, specifically designed for individuals with severe motor impairments, including those who are locked-in. Our approach goes beyond traditional accessibility — we're creating freedom.

Gaze-Based Navigation

Revolutionary eye-tracking technology that translates gaze patterns into seamless web navigation, eliminating the need for traditional input devices.

Intelligent Adaptation

AI-powered interface that learns and adapts to individual user patterns, optimizing the browsing experience for each person's unique needs.

Real-Time Processing

Lightning-fast processing ensures responsive interaction, making web browsing as natural and intuitive as looking around a room.

Human-Centered Design

Every aspect designed with dignity and independence in mind, empowering users to connect, learn, work, and explore without limitations.

For Locked-In Individuals

Our technology is particularly groundbreaking for individuals experiencing locked-in syndrome or similar conditions. We understand that physical constraints should never limit mental freedom or the ability to connect with the world.

CACTUS opens doors to education, employment, social connections, entertainment, and self-expression — enabling users to live full, connected lives regardless of physical limitations.
