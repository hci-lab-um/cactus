Breaking Barriers with Technology

CACTUS is a groundbreaking achievement in assistive technology, being the first of its kind at the University of Malta, a national milestone, and a significant step on the international stage.

Gaze-Based Navigation

Revolutionary eye-tracking technology that translates gaze patterns into seamless web navigation.

Adaptive Interfaces

Customizable interfaces tailored to individual user needs and preferences.

Switch Compatibility

Seamlessly integrates with adaptive switches for enhanced accessibility.

Inclusive Design

Built from the ground up to empower locked-in and mobility-impaired individuals.
